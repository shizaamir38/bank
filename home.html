Liberty Family Banking
Family Banking is a product in which individual members of a family can group their respective relationships (Savings Account, Fixed Deposit, Recurring Deposit) under a single ID called Family ID to cater to the whole Family Banking needs.
Help your entire family enjoy the advantages of our special services through Family Banking. Family member can be both NRE or Resident UAE customer. All you have to do is link your family members’ accounts with your Pro or Premia Account.

  Benefits of Family Banking

  • A single bank to all members in the family
  • Access to same privileges and benefits for the entire family
  • Lower minimum balance requirement at individual customer level
  • Flexibility to maintain balances across account
  • Single Relationship Manager to manage entire family’s banking needs

Variants of Family Banking
Pro Family Pro Account benefits to NRI family members and resident Titanium Account benefits to resident family members.  
Premia Family Premia Account benefits to NRI family members and resident Wealth Account benefits to resident family members.
